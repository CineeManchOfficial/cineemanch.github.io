description: "Ek shaadi-shuda aadmi… do ‘woh’… aur unlimited galatfehmiyaan… 😶 Sunne mein lagta hai full comedy blast hoga… lekin reality? Confusion zyada… comedy kam! 🤡

Story nayi nahi hai… aur honestly — presentation bhi itni engaging nahi hai ki aap bina bore hue reh pao 😬 Starting ke 15–20 minutes slow hain… jokes bhi utne land nahi karte… Aur sabse bada issue — film khud ko jitna funny samajhti hai… utni actually hai hi nahi. 😶

Ayushmann Khurrana? Talented actor hai… lekin yahan lagta hai jaise Dream Girl + Bala + Shubh Mangal ka remix chal raha ho! 🔁 Same awkward face… same bechara aadmi… same nervous husband… Aur kai scenes mein toh acting literally auto-mode pe chalti feel hoti hai! 🤖

Wamiqa Gabbi — finally koi toh hai jo real lagta hai 👏 Unke scenes mein honesty dikhti hai. 

Sara Ali Khan… 😶 Energy itni zyada hai ki acting aur overacting ke beech ki line hi gayab ho gayi! ⚡ Har scene aisa lagta hai jaise — “yeh reel viral honi chahiye!” 📱

Rakul Preet Singh — story mein hai… par bas naam ke liye 😐 Unka role sirf confusion badhane tak limited hai.

Aur screenplay? Bhai ek hi loop — 
❌ Galatfehmi 
❌ Safai 
❌ Phir nayi galatfehmi 
Aur yeh cycle poori film mein repeat hoti rehti hai 🔁 Comedy natural aane ke bajaye… forcefully daali hui lagti hai! UP accent aur desi flavour ke chakkar mein characters kabhi-kabhi insaan kam… caricature zyada lagte hain. 🎭 Film entertain karne ki koshish karti hai… lekin kai jagah bas shor machati hai, hasi nahi. 🔊

Supporting cast? 👇
Vijay Raaz — wonderful, par koi standout moment nahi 😕
Tigmanshu Dhulia — mature aur impactful! 🎯
Vishal Vashishth — confident & charming 😎
Guneet Singh & Durgesh Kumar — solid support, especially comic timing mein 😂
Lekin asli highlight? Ayesha Raza Mishra as bua! 🔥 Scene aate hi screen steal kar leti hain 👏
Baaki — Deepika Amin decent 👍 Shireesh Kumar Sharma ko scope nahi mila 😐 Aur Abhishek Sonpaliya apni presence feel kara dete hain ✔️

Overall — supporting cast ne film ko bachane ki poori koshish ki… par script unka full potential use nahi kar paayi 😶 Aur songs? Kahin bhi shurru ho jaate hain… story rok ke bas glamour show chalu! 💃

Final verdict? 🎬
Agar aap Ayushmann ke hardcore fan ho aur expectations low rakh ke ja rahe ho… toh ek baar dekh sakte ho 👍 Lekin agar aapko chahiye fresh comedy + smart writing… Toh bhai… yeh film sirf purane formula ko naye wrapper mein bech rahi hai. 🎁😶

बाकी… ये तो थी मेरी राय। अब आपकी बारी — कमेंट सेक्शन में ज़रूर बताइए। क्योंकि फिल्म का सच और असली फीचर… सब बताएगा जनता का मीटर!"
